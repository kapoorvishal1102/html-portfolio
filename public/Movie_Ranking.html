Movies I like the most this year.

The list is huge, but listing my top 3 movies

Carry On Jatta 3

This movie is all about for a guy to marry to love of his life.
I would say if you can understand punjabi, then it is must watch. This movie was 3 sequel for the same story line concept but 1st and 3rd only is good to watch.

Ani deya majak ee

This movie is all about finding true love after being physically handicapped.
In this, hero becomes blind and went to london to get his eye-sight treated to see the love of his life, whom he does not know where she lives.
Being a blind he met this girl and the girl falls for the guy based on his nature.

Mitran da naa chalda

This movie is all about girl looking for justice in the indian court against the falsely declared as prostitute as she was not ready to have sex with company owner, where she works.
The story revolves around her win against all the odds and triumph of justice.
